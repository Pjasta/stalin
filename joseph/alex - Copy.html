Famous Places in India

Place Description Image
Charminar Charminar is a famous monument located in Hyderabad. It was built in 1591 and is a popular tourist attraction.
Taj Mahal Taj Mahal is located in Agra. It was built by Shah Jahan and is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
India Gate India Gate is a war memorial located in New Delhi. It is one of the most famous landmarks in India.
Qutub Minar Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Delhi.
